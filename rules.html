STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

  • Anyone with an interest in cybersecurity, ethical hacking, and Capture the Flag (CTF) competitions is eligible to participate.
  • Teams must consist of 1-4 members (team members can be from different institutions or backgrounds).
  • All team members must be college students. No working professionals are allowed strictly.
  • Participants must have basic knowledge of cybersecurity, ethical hacking, and CTF competitions.
  • Each participant must provide a valid ID (government-issued ID, college ID, or any official identification) card during registration and check-in. Bring Aadhar Card, Pan Card, Driving License, Voter ID, or any other valid ID and 2 passport size photos with signature on the back.
  • All participants must agree to the event's code of conduct and fair play policies.

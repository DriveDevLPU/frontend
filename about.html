Who We Are

MISSION OVERVIEW

Drive Dev is a student-led tech club at Lovely Professional University that fosters AI, robotics, and automobile innovation.

Our mission is to create a collaborative space where students explore emerging technologies, develop technical skills, and connect with like-minded individuals.

OPERATIONAL STRATEGY

CORE OBJECTIVES

We empower students to think creatively, solve real-world problems, and contribute to technological advancements.

Our club thrives on teamwork, innovation, and continuous learning, ensuring every member grows professionally and personally.

Whether you are a beginner or an expert, Drive Dev offers a platform to experiment, learn, and build impactful projects.

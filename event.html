MISSION DETAILS

In the heart of a national digital battlefield, strategists from universities across the country gather, minds sharpened like blades. Each line of code becomes a potential weapon, every puzzle a fortified stronghold. Amidst the rapid echo of keystrokes and the electric buzz of anticipation, alliances form, rivals emerge, and the race for cyber dominance begins.

This is no ordinary competition—it's a test of intellect, skill, and resilience.

Tactics evolve on the fly as players navigate encrypted mazes, defuse logic bombs, and breach sophisticated firewalls, all within the high-stakes pressure of this 24-hour World War-themed Capture the Flag (CTF). The battlefield stretches far and wide—every flag captured a hard-earned victory, every exploit uncovered a step closer to total domination.

As the hours tick away, adversaries push the limits of their knowledge, attacking vulnerabilities with precision and defending their ground with unwavering resolve. In this relentless pursuit of mastery, no move is trivial, and every second counts.
